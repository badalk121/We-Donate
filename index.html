About We Donate

How we came up with it?

Last year, we witnessed the large mass of daily wagers struggling to reach their home after our government imposed complete lockdown. It was a painful sight to watch them travel hundreds of kilometers with their children. So, we decided that we should make some efforts to lighten their problems a bit.

Facts And Figures

Two-thirds of people in India live in poverty: 68.8% of the Indian population lives on less than $2 a day. Over 30% even have less than $1.25 per day available - they are considered extremely poor. This makes the Indian subcontinent one of the poorest countries in the world; women and children, the weakest members of Indian society, suffer most.

Why You Should Donate?

Every day, we come through people who don't have proper food to eat, don't have a place to stay, clothes, and some don't even know who they are due to their mental state now. So a little bit of affirmation and some supplies will be enough to help them.

Is "We Donate" reliable?

It is absolutely reliable as we are going to set this oraganisation virtually as well as physically. But due to the current Covid-19 situations in India, it is pretty hard to get the valid and neccessary documents ready. But when we are done with them, we will begin with our services.

How are we going to deliver?

As soon as we hit a certain amount of donation in terms of money and goods, we will utilise them to help the needy ones. Our donators can keep a check in the "Explore Our Work" section, where we will post pictures and expenditures details to ensure transparency.
